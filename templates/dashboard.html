Under the hood
The signal mix powering both backends.
Elo Engine

FiveThirtyEight-style ratings: K=20 update factor, +100 home court advantage, margin-of-victory multiplier with diminishing returns, season-boundary regression toward the league mean.

Four Factors

Dean Oliver's framework: effective FG%, turnover rate, offensive rebound rate, free-throw rate. Both offensive and defensive sides — captures forced-TOV and rebound-prevention quality.

Sequence Encoder

Two-tower Transformer with shared weights ingests each team's last 20 games. Pre-norm encoder, [CLS] pooling, multi-task win + score head. The natural backbone for the upcoming diffusion outcome model.
